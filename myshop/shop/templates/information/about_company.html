PetsLove is a pet store chain that was founded in 1960 in Whittier, California. The store was originally called Love's Pet Shop and was a small pet store that sold products for dogs and cats. Over time, the store expanded its product line to include aquatic products, bird products, and small animal products. In 1987, the store was renamed PetsLove and began to offer a wider range of animals and products. The pet store has remained a family-owned business, with the founder's granddaughter, Christine Chang, taking over as CEO and creative director in 2010. Under Christine's leadership, PetsLove has undergone several upgrades and renovations. In 2015, the store's new owners announced plans for new animals and exhibits, with facilities upgrades first on the list. PetsLove is known for its commitment to animal welfare and education. The pet store offers up-close animal adventures and interactive areas for visitors to enjoy. The store also hosts events throughout the year, such as the annual Pet Expo event, where kids can enjoy the store and learn about animals. PetsLove has become a popular destination for families and animal lovers alike. The pet store and zoo continue to expand their product lines and services, with a focus on providing visitors with a unique and memorable experience.
